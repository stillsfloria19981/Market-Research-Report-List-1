<p><strong>The IGZO Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amorphous IGZO</li><li>Crystalline IGZO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IGZO display market is primarily divided into two types: Amorphous IGZO and Crystalline IGZO. Amorphous IGZO, characterized by its non-crystalline structure, offers advantages such as greater flexibility and easier fabrication, making it suitable for lightweight and curved displays. In contrast, Crystalline IGZO features a more orderly arrangement of atoms, resulting in higher electron mobility and superior display performance, ideal for high-resolution screens. Both types cater to different applications, including smartphones, tablets, and large-sized TVs.</p></p>
