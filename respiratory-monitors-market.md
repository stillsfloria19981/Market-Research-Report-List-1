<p><p>The respiratory monitors market consists of various types that aid in assessing lung function and oxygen levels. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen saturation levels non-invasively, providing critical data for respiratory care. Incentive spirometers encourage patients to take deep breaths, promoting lung expansion and preventing complications. Peak flow meters assess air flow in the lungs, helping to manage conditions like asthma. Other market segments may include devices such as capnometers and handheld spirometers, supporting comprehensive respiratory monitoring.</p></p>
