<p><p>The floating ball valves market is categorized into three main types: manual, electric, and pneumatic. Manual floating ball valves are operated by hand, providing straightforward control for on/off services. Electric floating ball valves utilize electric actuators for remote or automated operations, enhancing efficiency and precision. Pneumatic floating ball valves are driven by compressed air, offering quick response and ease of integration into automated systems. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, ensuring effective fluid management and control.</p></p>
