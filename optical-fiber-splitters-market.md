<p><p>The optical fiber splitters market is divided into Type I and Type II segments. In contrast, Type II splitters are designed for more complex network configurations, supporting multiple input and output channels. They are often utilized in data centers and telecom infrastructure, providing flexibility and scalability for high-capacity networks. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing communication efficiency.</p></p>
