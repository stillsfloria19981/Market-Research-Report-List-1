<p><ul><li>Magnetic Resistance</li><li>Felt Pad Resistance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indoor cycling bike market primarily features two types of resistance systems: magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance. Magnetic resistance utilizes magnets to provide a smooth, adjustable workout experience with minimal wear and noise, promoting longevity and quiet operation. In contrast, felt pad resistance employs a friction-based system where a felt pad presses against the flywheel, offering a more traditional cycling feel but can generate more noise and wear over time. Each type caters to different user preferences and workout intensities.</p></p>
