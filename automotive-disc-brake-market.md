<p><strong>The Automotive Disc Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opposed Piston</li><li>Floating Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive disc brake market includes mainly two types: opposed piston and floating caliper systems. Opposed piston brakes feature pistons on both sides of the rotor, providing balanced braking force and enhanced performance, often used in high-performance vehicles. In contrast, floating calipers employ a single or fixed piston that moves the caliper to create braking pressure, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. Both systems are crucial for vehicle safety and performance, catering to various automotive applications.</p></p>
