<p><p>The forklift ramp market comprises two main types: manual forklift ramps and automatic forklift ramps. Manual forklift ramps are operated by personnel, requiring manual setup and positioning, making them suitable for simple applications and lighter loads. In contrast, automatic forklift ramps feature mechanized systems for deployment and retraction, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs, ideal for high-traffic environments. Both types serve the essential function of facilitating the safe loading and unloading of goods between different levels.</p></p>
