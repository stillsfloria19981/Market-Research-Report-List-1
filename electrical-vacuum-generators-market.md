<p><p>The Electrical Vacuum Generators market is primarily categorized into two types: single-stage and multi-stage vacuum generators. Single-stage vacuum generators utilize a single pumping mechanism to create vacuum, making them suitable for applications requiring lower vacuum levels and simpler operations. In contrast, multi-stage vacuum generators consist of multiple interconnected pumping mechanisms, allowing them to achieve higher vacuum levels and handle more complex tasks. This differentiation caters to various industrial needs, from basic vacuum applications to advanced processes requiring enhanced performance.</p></p>
