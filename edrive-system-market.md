<p><p>The eDrive system market comprises three primary types: 3-in-1 systems, 2-in-1 systems, and single modules. 3-in-1 systems integrate the electric motor, inverter, and transmission into a compact unit, enhancing efficiency and space utilization. 2-in-1 systems combine the electric motor and inverter, offering a balance between performance and complexity. Single modules focus on isolated components, allowing for versatility and easier integration into various vehicle designs. Each type addresses specific needs in electric and hybrid vehicle applications, driving technological advancements.</p></p>
