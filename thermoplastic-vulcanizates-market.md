<p><p>Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPVs) are advanced materials combining elastomers with thermoplastics, particularly in the context of EPDM/PP and NR/PP blends. EPDM/PP blends feature ethylene-propylene-diene monomer vulcanized rubber mixed with polypropylene, offering excellent weather resistance and recyclability. In contrast, NR/PP blends combine natural rubber with polypropylene, providing enhanced elasticity and impact resistance. Both types are utilized in automotive, consumer goods, and industrial applications, valued for their durability, processing ease, and ability to meet demanding performance requirements.</p></p>
