<p><p>The COVID-19 IgG/IgM Rapid Test Cassette market consists of three primary types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using a simple cassette format, suitable for point-of-care settings. ELISA tests, while more complex, offer high sensitivity and specificity for detecting antibodies in larger sample volumes. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, providing insight into immune response and potential vaccine efficacy, essential for research and clinical applications.</p></p>
