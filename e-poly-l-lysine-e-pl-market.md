<p><p>ε-Poly-L-lysine (ε-PL) is a natural, biodegradable polymer derived from the amino acid L-lysine, commonly produced through fermentation by certain bacteria. Additionally, ε-PL is utilized in pharmaceuticals for drug delivery and as a stabilizer in various formulations.</p><p>The ε-poly-L-lysine market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing consumer demand for natural preservatives and the rising trend of clean-label products. The food and beverage sector is the largest contributor to this growth, particularly in processed foods and beverages, where ε-PL is valued for its effectiveness in extending shelf life.</p><p>The ε-poly-L-lysine market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period.
