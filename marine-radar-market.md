<p><ul><li>X Band Radar</li><li>S Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine radar market is primarily segmented into X Band and S Band radar systems. X Band radars operate at higher frequencies, offering greater detail and better target resolution, making them ideal for close-range navigation and detecting smaller objects like buoys. In contrast, S Band radars function at lower frequencies, providing longer range detection and better performance in severe weather conditions, making them suited for open-sea navigation and larger vessels. Both types serve distinct purposes in maritime operations.</p></p>
