Granules offer precise dosing and are easy to handle, often used in specific dyeing processes. Paste colourants provide concentrated solutions that facilitate uniform application, particularly on fabrics. Liquid colourants are ready-to-use formulations, widely preferred for their ease of mixing and application, catering to diverse textile dyeing needs across the industry.</p></p>
