<p><ul><li>Normally Open</li><li>Normally Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety monitoring relay market consists of two primary types: normally open (NO) and normally closed (NC) relays. Normally open relays require an external signal to close the circuit, ensuring safety features activate only when needed. Conversely, normally closed relays maintain a closed circuit in a default state, providing immediate safety actions when power is interrupted or a fault occurs. These types are critical for various industrial applications, ensuring machine safety and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
