The "Button" dampener is a small, round accessory that fits between the strings, reducing vibrations upon ball impact and providing a softer feel. The "Worm" dampener features a long, flexible design that weaves through the strings, offering a more significant vibration reduction and enhanced control. Both types are popular among players seeking improved comfort and performance on the court.</p></p>
