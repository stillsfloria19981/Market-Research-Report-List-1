<p><p>Bamboo-based flooring comes in several types, including solid bamboo floor, engineered bamboo floor, strand woven bamboo floor, and other variants. Solid bamboo flooring is crafted from whole bamboo stalks, offering durability and a natural appearance. Engineered bamboo flooring consists of a bamboo surface over a plywood base, enhancing moisture resistance. Strand woven bamboo flooring is made from compressed bamboo fibers, creating a dense and resilient surface. Other market types may include laminated or textured options, catering to diverse aesthetic and performance needs.</p></p>
