<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The XLPE Cable & Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LV Cables</li><li>MV Cables</li><li>HV Cables</li><li>EHV Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) cables and wires are categorized into four main types based on voltage levels: </p><p>1. **LV Cables** (Low Voltage) are used for residential and commercial applications, typically up to 1 kV.</p><p>2. **MV Cables** (Medium Voltage) range from 1 kV to 36 kV and serve industrial and utility infrastructure.</p><p>3. **HV Cables** (High Voltage) operate between 36 kV and 150 kV, crucial for long-distance power transmission.</p><p>4. **EHV Cables** (Extra High Voltage) exceed 150 kV, essential for large-scale electricity distribution networks.</p></p>
