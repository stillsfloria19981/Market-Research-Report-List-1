<p><p>An Auto Transformer Rectifier Unit (ATRU) is an electrical device that converts alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) while efficiently managing voltage levels. ATRUs are categorized based on the number of pulses they generate: 6 Pulse, 12 Pulse, and 18 Pulse. The 6 Pulse design is simpler and cost-effective but has higher harmonic distortion. The 12 Pulse configuration offers improved power quality with reduced harmonics, while the 18 Pulse design further enhances efficiency and power stability, suitable for demanding applications.</p></p>
