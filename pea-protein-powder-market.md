<p><p>The pea protein powder market primarily consists of two types: pea protein isolates and pea protein concentrates. Pea protein isolates undergo extensive processing to remove most fats and carbohydrates, resulting in a product that is approximately 80-90% protein, making it ideal for high-protein formulations. In contrast, pea protein concentrates retain more nutrients and are about 70-85% protein, offering a more balanced nutritional profile. Both types cater to the growing demand for plant-based protein in food, beverages, and supplement industries.</p></p>
