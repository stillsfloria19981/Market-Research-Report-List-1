<p><p>Proportional flow control valves regulate fluid flow and pressure in various applications. Electric proportional valves use electrical signals to adjust the flow based on demand, offering precise control and rapid response in systems like automotive and industrial machinery. Hydraulic proportional valves, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic pressure to modulate flow, making them ideal for high-force applications such as construction equipment and manufacturing processes. Both types enhance efficiency and performance in fluid control systems, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
