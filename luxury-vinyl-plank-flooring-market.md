<p><p>The luxury vinyl plank flooring market is divided into two main types: flexible and rigid vinyl plank flooring. Flexible vinyl plank flooring is softer and more adaptable, making it suitable for uneven surfaces and easier to install. In contrast, rigid vinyl plank flooring is characterized by a sturdy core, providing enhanced durability and stability, often designed to mimic hardwood. Both types offer a range of styles and finishes, catering to diverse consumer preferences and applications in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
