<p><p>The integrated circuit leadframe market comprises two primary types based on manufacturing processes: stamping and etching. Stamping process leadframes are produced through mechanical pressing, allowing for high-speed production and uniformity, making them suitable for large volume applications. In contrast, etching process leadframes involve chemical removal of material to create intricate designs, offering greater precision for complex geometries. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor packaging, impacting performance and reliability in electronics.</p></p>
