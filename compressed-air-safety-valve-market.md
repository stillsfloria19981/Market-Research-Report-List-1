<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Safety Valves</li><li>Solenoid Driven Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressed air safety valve market comprises two main types: pneumatic safety valves and solenoid-driven safety valves. Pneumatic safety valves utilize air pressure to control airflow and activate safety mechanisms, ensuring system protection during overpressure situations. In contrast, solenoid-driven safety valves rely on electromagnetic coils to operate, providing rapid response times and precise control. Both types are essential for maintaining safety in industrial applications, preventing equipment damage, and ensuring the safe operation of compressed air systems.</p></p>
