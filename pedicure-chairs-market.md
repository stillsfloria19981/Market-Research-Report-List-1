<p><ul><li>Piped-in Type</li><li>Pipeless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pedicure chairs market can be categorized into two main types: piped-in and pipeless. Piped-in pedicure chairs feature built-in plumbing systems that allow for water circulation through pipes, providing a traditional spa experience. However, they require more maintenance due to potential plumbing issues. In contrast, pipeless pedicure chairs utilize a jet system without plumbing, making them easier to clean and more hygienic. Both types have unique benefits, catering to different preferences and operational requirements in salons and spas.</p></p>
