<p><p>Tidal energy power stations can be categorized based on their operational design in relation to water movement. </p><p>1. **Single Warehouse One-Way Power Station**: Utilizes tidal flow in one direction to generate energy, with water released only during ebb tides.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Single Warehouse Two-Way Power Station**: Captures energy from both incoming and outgoing tides, enhancing efficiency by operating in both directions. </p><p>3. **Double Warehouse Two-Way Power Station**: Features two separate chambers, enabling simultaneous tidal flow energy capture, maximizing output through refined design and operation.</p></p>
