<p><p>The desiccant inserters market is segmented into two main types: canister and pouch. Pouch inserters, on the other hand, employ flexible pouches filled with desiccants, offering lightweight and space-efficient solutions for smaller items. Both types play a crucial role in preserving product integrity by controlling humidity levels, thereby enhancing shelf life and maintaining quality in various industries.</p></p>
