<p><p>The concrete dye market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based dyes. Water-based dyes are environmentally friendly, easy to apply, and penetrate the surface effectively, offering vibrant colors and quick drying times. Conversely, solvent-based dyes are known for their durability and resistance to fading, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. Each type serves distinct purposes, with water-based options preferred for residential projects and solvent-based options often used in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
