<p><ul><li>On-premises</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Airfield Management System market is categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises systems are installed directly on an airport's local servers, providing full control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems operate on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as they allow users to access data from anywhere with internet connectivity. Both options cater to different operational needs and preferences in the aviation industry.</p></p>
