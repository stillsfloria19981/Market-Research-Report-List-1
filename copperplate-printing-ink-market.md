<p><p>The Copperplate Printing Ink market includes three main types: solvent-borne ink, water-borne ink, and oil-based ink. Solvent-borne ink uses organic solvents, offering quick drying and vibrant colors, making it suitable for various surfaces. Water-borne ink is environmentally friendly, utilizing water as a primary solvent, ideal for sensitive applications. Oil-based ink, known for its rich consistency, provides excellent adhesion and durability, commonly used in traditional printing methods. Each type caters to specific printing needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
