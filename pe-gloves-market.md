<p><strong>The PE Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE)</li><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Ultra-high Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PE gloves market comprises various types of polyethylene materials, each offering distinct characteristics. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) gloves are flexible and comfortable, ideal for light-duty tasks. Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) gloves provide a balance of strength and flexibility, suitable for moderate protection. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) gloves are more robust and offer better puncture resistance, perfect for heavy-duty applications. Ultra-high Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) gloves offer exceptional durability and cut resistance, making them suitable for industrial and high-risk environments.</p></p>
