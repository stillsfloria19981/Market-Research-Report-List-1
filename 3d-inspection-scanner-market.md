<p><p>The 3D inspection scanner market comprises several key types, including laser 3D scanners, structured light 3D scanners, and others. Laser 3D scanners utilize laser beams to capture precise measurements of objects, providing high accuracy and detail, ideal for industrial applications. Structured light 3D scanners project light patterns onto surfaces to gather dimensional data, making them suitable for quality control and reverse engineering. The "others" category includes alternative technologies such as photogrammetry and contact-based measurement systems, catering to various inspection needs.</p></p>
