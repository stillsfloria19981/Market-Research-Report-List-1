<p><p>The Electrical Digital Twin market encompasses four primary types: Parts Twin, Product Twin, Process Twin, and System Twin. Parts Twin focuses on individual components, providing detailed performance data. Product Twin represents entire products, integrating multiple parts for comprehensive analysis. Process Twin optimizes manufacturing and operational workflows by simulating processes in real-time. System Twin encompasses networks of interconnected systems, enabling holistic monitoring and optimization across entire infrastructures. Each type aids in enhancing efficiency, reducing costs, and improving decision-making in electrical applications.</p></p>
