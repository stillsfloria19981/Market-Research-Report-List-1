<p><strong>The Jigsaw Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Reciprocating Jigsaw</li><li>Orbital Reciprocating Jigsaw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jigsaw tools market can be classified into two main types: straight reciprocating jigsaws and orbital reciprocating jigsaws. Straight reciprocating jigsaws are designed for precision cutting, offering clean, straight cuts in various materials. In contrast, orbital reciprocating jigsaws incorporate a pendulum motion that enhances cutting efficiency and speed, making them ideal for faster, rough cuts. Both types cater to different user needs in construction and woodworking, impacting overall performance based on the specific cutting tasks required.</p></p>
