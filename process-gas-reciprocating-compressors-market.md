<p><p>Process gas reciprocating compressors are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types based on the number of compression stages utilized for gas compression. Single-stage compressors are designed for low-pressure applications, compressing gas in one cycle, ideal for simple tasks and lower capacities. In contrast, multi-stage compressors use multiple cylinders and stages to achieve higher pressures efficiently, making them suitable for more demanding applications in industries like petrochemical, natural gas, and chemical processing, where high-pressure gas delivery is required.</p></p>
