<p><p>The Residential Tree Maintenance Services market includes two primary types: Corrective Maintenance and Preventive Maintenance. Corrective Maintenance involves addressing existing tree issues, such as disease treatment, pruning for safety, or removal of hazardous trees. In contrast, Preventive Maintenance focuses on proactive measures to maintain tree health and prevent future problems, including regular inspections, fertilization, and pest management. Both approaches aim to enhance the longevity and safety of trees, ensuring healthy residential landscapes while minimizing risks associated with tree deterioration.</p></p>
