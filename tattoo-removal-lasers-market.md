<p><p>The tattoo removal laser market includes several key types: Q-Switched lasers, Picosecond lasers, and Combination lasers. Q-Switched lasers are effective for breaking down tattoo ink with short, high-energy pulses, making them a popular choice. Picosecond lasers offer even faster pulse durations, leading to quicker ink destruction and reduced skin damage. Combination lasers leverage the strengths of both types, utilizing multiple wavelengths to target a broader range of ink colors and enhance overall treatment efficacy, thereby improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
