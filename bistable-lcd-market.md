<p><strong>The Bistable LCD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>COG Chip</li><li>COF Chip</li><li>COB Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bistable LCD market comprises three primary types: COG (Chip-on-Glass), COF (Chip-on-Flex), and COB (Chip-on-Board). COG integrates the driver chip directly onto the glass substrate, offering a compact design ideal for space-constrained applications. COF bonds the driver chip to a flexible substrate, allowing for versatile layout options, particularly in curved or portable devices. COB mounts the driver directly to a printed circuit board, providing robust performance in environments requiring durable displays. Each type caters to different application needs and design specifications.</p></p>
