<p><ul><li>P Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polysilicon wafer market is primarily divided into two types: P-type and N-type wafers. P-type wafers are doped with elements like boron, creating positive charge carriers (holes) and are widely used in photovoltaic cells due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. N-type wafers, doped with phosphorus, generate negative charge carriers (electrons) and offer higher efficiency and better performance in high-temperature conditions. Both types play crucial roles in the solar energy sector, catering to diverse application needs in the industry.</p></p>
