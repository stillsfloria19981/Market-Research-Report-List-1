<p><p>A Reluctance Synchronous Motor (RSM) is a type of electric motor that operates based on the principle of magnetic reluctance, where the rotor aligns with the stator's magnetic field. Unlike traditional synchronous motors that rely on permanent magnets, RSMs use varying reluctance in the rotor to achieve synchronization with the rotating magnetic field of the stator. This makes them efficient and suitable for applications where high torque and precise control are required.</p><p>The Reluctance Synchronous Motor Market is witnessing significant growth driven by advancements in technology, increasing demand for energy-efficient systems, and the rising adoption of electric vehicles.
