<p><p>Engineered quartz countertops, commonly known as E-Quartz, are made from a blend of natural quartz and resins. The market features two primary types: phaneromer quartz and cryptocrystalline quartz. Phaneromer quartz consists of larger, visible crystal grains, offering a striking appearance and varied textures. In contrast, cryptocrystalline quartz is composed of much finer crystals, resulting in a smooth and uniform surface. Both types are popular for their durability, versatility, and aesthetic appeal in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
