<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Maritime Thermal Imaging Cameras market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging cameras. Uncooled thermal cameras operate at ambient temperature, utilizing materials like microbolometers to detect infrared radiation. They are cost-effective and lightweight, making them suitable for various maritime applications. In contrast, cooled thermal cameras refrigerate their detectors to enhance sensitivity and image clarity, ideal for long-range and high-performance needs in challenging maritime environments. Both types serve crucial roles in surveillance, navigation, and search-and-rescue operations.</p></p>
