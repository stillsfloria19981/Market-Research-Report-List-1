<p><p>The benchtop ultracentrifuge market is segmented into two main types: analytical and preparative ultracentrifuges. Analytical ultracentrifuges are used primarily for research and analysis, allowing scientists to study the properties and behavior of macromolecules and particles in solution through techniques like sedimentation velocity and sedimentation equilibrium. Preparative ultracentrifuges, on the other hand, are designed for sample separation and purification, efficiently isolating biological molecules, cells, or subcellular components for further analysis or applications in laboratories and bioprocessing.</p></p>
