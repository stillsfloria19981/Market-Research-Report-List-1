<p><p>Halogen-free control cables are specialized electrical cables designed for use in environments where low smoke and toxic gas emissions are critical in the event of a fire. These cables are made from materials that do not release halogens, such as chlorine and bromine, which are known to produce harmful gases when burned. This characteristic enhances safety in buildings, tunnels, and other public spaces, making halogen-free cables a preferred choice for many industries, including construction, automotive, and energy.</p><p>The Halogen-free Control Cable Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period. Innovations in cable manufacturing and the development of new formulations to enhance performance are further propelling the market. Key types include LSZH (Low Smoke Zero Halogen) cables, which minimize harmful emissions during a fire, making them ideal for indoor use in public spaces. Both types are increasingly preferred in sectors such as construction, transportation, and industrial applications for improved safety standards.</p></p>
