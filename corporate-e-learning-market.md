<p><p>The corporate e-learning market can be categorized into two main deployment types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise deployment involves installing e-learning software on internal servers, allowing for greater control over data and customization but requiring significant IT resources and infrastructure. In contrast, cloud-based deployment utilizes external servers hosted by a service provider, enabling easy access, scalability, and lower upfront costs. This model offers flexibility and automatic updates, making it a popular choice for many organizations seeking efficient training solutions.</p></p>
