<p><p>ICT probes are essential tools in the electronics testing market, specifically in the context of in-circuit testing. Short stroke test probes are designed for quick, precision contact with circuit points, allowing efficient testing without extensive movement, ideal for densely packed PCBs. Long stroke test probes, on the other hand, provide greater reach and flexibility, accommodating larger or more complex assemblies and ensuring reliable connections. Both types play critical roles in ensuring product quality and performance in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
