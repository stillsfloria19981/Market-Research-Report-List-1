<p><p>The telescopic belt conveyor market can be categorized into two main types: fixed type and movable type. Fixed type conveyors are installed in a specific location, providing a permanent solution for material handling within warehouses and distribution centers. In contrast, movable type conveyors are designed for flexibility, allowing easy repositioning and use in various locations for loading and unloading tasks. Both types enhance efficiency in logistics operations by streamlining the transfer of goods, reducing manual labor, and improving workflow.</p></p>
