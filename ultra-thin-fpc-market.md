<p><p>The ultra-thin flexible printed circuit (FPC) market is primarily segmented into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided FPCs have conductive patterns on one side, making them suitable for simple designs with limited space and lower costs. In contrast, double-sided FPCs feature conductive patterns on both sides, allowing for more complex circuitry and higher functionality. This versatility enables their use in various applications, including smartphones, wearables, and automotive electronics, catering to the growing demand for compact and lightweight electronic solutions.</p></p>
