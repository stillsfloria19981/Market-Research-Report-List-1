<p><strong>The Connected Motorcycles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular V2X</li><li>Dedicated Short Range Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Connected Motorcycles market encompasses two primary communication technologies: Cellular V2X and Dedicated Short-Range Communication (DSRC). Cellular V2X utilizes existing cellular networks to facilitate vehicle-to-everything communication, enhancing safety and navigation through real-time data exchange. In contrast, DSRC is a short-range communication protocol designed for high-speed vehicle communication, allowing instant transmission of safety messages between bikes and roadside infrastructure. Both technologies aim to improve traffic safety, efficiency, and the overall riding experience by enabling enhanced connectivity between motorcycles and their environment.</p></p>
