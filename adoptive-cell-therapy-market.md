<p><p>Adoptive Cell Therapy encompasses various approaches to enhance immune responses against cancer. CAR-T (Chimeric Antigen Receptor T-cell) therapy involves engineering a patient's T-cells to target specific tumor antigens. TCR-T (T-cell Receptor Therapy) utilizes modified T-cells to recognize and attack intracellular antigens. NK (Natural Killer) cell therapy employs activated NK cells to eliminate tumors without prior sensitization. TIL (Tumor Infiltrating Lymphocyte) therapy involves extracting and expanding T-cells directly from a patient's tumor to fight cancer more effectively. Each type targets cancer through distinct mechanisms, enhancing therapeutic options.</p></p>
