<p><p>The fire-resistant cable material market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene)** delivers excellent thermal resistance and electrical insulation, ideal for high-voltage applications.</p><p>2. **LSZH (Low Smoke Zero Halogen)** materials emit minimal smoke and no halogen gases when burned, enhancing safety in enclosed spaces.</p><p>3. **PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)** is widely used for its good balance of cost and performance, though it releases toxic fumes when ignited.</p><p>4. **EPR (Ethylene Propylene Rubber)** offers exceptional flexibility and thermal stability, suitable for harsh environments. Each type serves specific applications based on fire safety requirements.</p></p>
