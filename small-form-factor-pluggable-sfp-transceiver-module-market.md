<p><p>The Small Form-factor Pluggable (SFP) transceiver module market includes two primary types: Singlemode Fiber (SMF) and Multimode Fiber (MMF). Singlemode Fiber modules transmit data over long distances with minimal signal loss, ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. In contrast, Multimode Fiber modules support shorter distances and are commonly used in local area networks (LANs) due to their cost-effectiveness. Both types facilitate flexibility and scalability in data communication, serving diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
