<p><p>The carbon steel rebars market comprises primarily two types: deformed steel and mild steel. Deformed steel rebars feature surface patterns that improve their bond with concrete, enhancing structural integrity, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Mild steel rebars, in contrast, have a smooth surface and are more ductile, making them suitable for low-strength projects and non-load bearing structures. Together, these types cater to various construction needs, reflecting a balance of strength, flexibility, and application-specific demands.</p></p>
