<p><p>Zirconium heat exchangers are categorized into several types based on their design and application. Tube-type heat exchangers feature a series of tubes where one fluid flows through, and heat is exchanged with another fluid flowing around the tubes, offering easy maintenance. Other types may include panel or plate exchangers, each designed for specific operational requirements and efficiency needs in various industries.</p></p>
