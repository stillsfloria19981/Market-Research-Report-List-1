<p><strong>The Micro-single Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mirrorless System</li><li>Fixed Mirror System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-single camera market includes two primary types: mirrorless systems and fixed mirror systems. Mirrorless systems utilize an electronic viewfinder and lack a traditional mirror mechanism, allowing for more compact designs and faster autofocus. In contrast, fixed mirror systems, often resembling DSLRs, incorporate a fixed mirror that reflects light into an optical viewfinder, offering a bulkier build but enhanced optics and performance. Both types cater to various photography enthusiasts, balancing portability, image quality, and feature sets.</p></p>
