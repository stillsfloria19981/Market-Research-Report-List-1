RF sputtering operates at radio frequencies, allowing for the deposition of insulating materials by providing sufficient energy to dislodge atoms. Other methods may include magnetron sputtering or ultra-high vacuum techniques, each tailored for specific applications and materials, enhancing versatility in thin film deposition across various industries.</p></p>
