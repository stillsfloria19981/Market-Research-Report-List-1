Despite the growing popularity of radial tires, bias tires remain relevant in specific sectors due to their robustness and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>The Car Bias Tire Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period. However, the market faces challenges from the rising adoption of radial tires, which offer better fuel efficiency and longer tread life.</p><p>Recent trends in the Car Bias Tire Market include advancements in tire materials and manufacturing processes to improve performance and reduce environmental impact. <p><p>The **Car Bias Tire Market** includes two main types: **Ordinary Bias Tires** and **Bias Belted Tires**. Ordinary Bias Tires feature crisscrossed ply layers, offering durability and affordability, ideal for heavy-duty or off-road vehicles. Bias Belted Tires add a stabilizing belt layer beneath the tread, enhancing stability, speed, and comfort while maintaining durability. Both types cater to specific needs, with Ordinary Bias Tires excelling in rugged conditions and Bias Belted Tires balancing performance and longevity for varied driving environments.</p></p>
