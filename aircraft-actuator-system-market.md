<p><strong>The Aircraft Actuator System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-Hydraulic Actuator</li><li>Electro-Mechanical Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Actuator System market encompasses two primary types: Electro-Hydraulic Actuators and Electro-Mechanical Actuators. Electro-Hydraulic Actuators utilize hydraulic fluid to create force, providing high power-to-weight ratios and precise control, making them suitable for larger aircraft systems like flight control and landing gear. In contrast, Electro-Mechanical Actuators convert electrical energy directly into mechanical motion, offering simplicity and reliability, ideal for applications where weight and maintenance are critical. Both types play pivotal roles in enhancing aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
