<p><p>The hunting crossbow market is primarily divided into two types: compound and recurve crossbows. Compound crossbows utilize a system of pulleys and cables to increase power and reduce effort for the shooter, making them more efficient for rapid shots. In contrast, recurve crossbows feature a traditional design with limbs that curve away from the shooter, offering simplicity and ease of use. Both types cater to different preferences, with compound models favored for advanced performance and recurve models appreciated for their classic appeal.</p></p>
