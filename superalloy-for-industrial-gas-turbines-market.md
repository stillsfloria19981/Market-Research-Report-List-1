<p><p>The superalloy market for industrial gas turbines is categorized into three primary types: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Iron-based superalloys offer good mechanical strength and oxidation resistance but are limited by temperature thresholds. Nickel-based superalloys are favored for their exceptional high-temperature performance and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for turbine components. Cobalt-based superalloys provide excellent wear resistance and stability under extreme conditions, often used in applications requiring robust performance. Each type serves specific needs in turbine efficiency and durability.</p></p>
