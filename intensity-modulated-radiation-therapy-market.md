<p><p>Intensity Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT) is an advanced form of radiation therapy used to treat cancer. It allows for the delivery of precise radiation doses to targeted tumors while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissues. IMRT utilizes computer-controlled linear accelerators to optimize the radiation beams, tailoring the intensity and shape according to the specific characteristics of the tumor. This technique enhances treatment efficacy and reduces side effects, making it a preferred choice in cancer management.</p><p>The Intensity Modulated Radiation Therapy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15.00% during the forecast period, driven by increasing cancer incidences globally and technological advancements in radiotherapy.
