<p><p>The sintered NdFeB magnet market is categorized into several types based on coercivity levels, which signify the magnet's resistance to demagnetization. Low Coercivity (N) magnets are easier to demagnetize but are less expensive. Medium Coercivity (M) magnets offer a balance between performance and cost. High Coercivity (H) magnets provide better stability and are suitable for demanding applications. Ultra-high Coercivity (SH and UH) magnets excel in extreme conditions, offering superior performance in specialized fields, such as aerospace and electric vehicles.</p></p>
