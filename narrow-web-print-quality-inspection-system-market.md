<p><p>The Narrow Web Print Quality Inspection System market encompasses two main types: in-line and off-line inspection systems. In-line print quality inspection systems are integrated directly into the printing process, allowing real-time monitoring and immediate corrections, ensuring high-quality output without disrupting production. Off-line print quality inspection systems, on the other hand, operate separately from the printing process, where printed materials are evaluated after production, enabling comprehensive analysis and detailed quality checks before distribution. Both types enhance product integrity and customer satisfaction.</p></p>
