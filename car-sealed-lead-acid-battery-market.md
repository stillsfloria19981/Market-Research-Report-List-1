<p><strong>The Car Sealed Lead-acid Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Sealed Lead-Acid (SLA) Battery market includes AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries use fiberglass mats to hold electrolyte, offering high power output, durability, and vibration resistance, making them ideal for start-stop vehicles and high-demand applications. Gel batteries use a silica-based gel electrolyte, providing deep-cycle capabilities, low self-discharge, and better performance in extreme temperatures, suited for long-term, low-maintenance use. Both are maintenance-free, spill-proof, and widely used in automotive and backup power systems, with AGM being more common in modern vehicles.</p></p>
