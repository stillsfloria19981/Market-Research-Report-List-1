<p><p>Low Temperature Poly-Silicon (LTPS) displays are advanced flat panel display technologies that utilize a silicon-based thin-film transistor (TFT) process at lower temperatures. LTPS displays are widely used in smartphones, tablets, and high-end televisions, driving significant interest in the market.</p><p>The Low Temperature Poly-Silicon (LTPS) Display Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. Additionally, the ongoing trend towards miniaturization and higher resolutions in electronic devices favors the adoption of LTPS technology. Recent developments include improvements in manufacturing processes and the integration of LTPS displays with OLED technology, further expanding their applications. As the market continues to evolve, there is a strong potential for LTPS displays to play a vital role in the next generation of electronic devices, keeping pace with consumer expectations for quality and performance.</p></p>
