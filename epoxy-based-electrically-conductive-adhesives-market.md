<p><strong>The Epoxy Based Electrically Conductive Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic</li><li>Anisotropic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy-based electrically conductive adhesives market is divided into isotropic and anisotropic types. Isotropic adhesives provide uniform electrical conductivity in all directions, making them suitable for various applications requiring consistent performance, such as potting and encapsulation. Anisotropic adhesives, on the other hand, offer conductivity primarily in one direction, which is advantageous for specific applications like flexible circuits and interconnecting substrates. Both types are critical in electronics assembly, enhancing reliability and performance in devices.</p></p>
