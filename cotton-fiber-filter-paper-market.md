<p><p>The cotton fiber filter paper market is segmented into qualitative and quantitative filter paper types. Qualitative filter paper is used primarily for general laboratory filtration, offering a range of porosities for various applications, such as separating solids from liquids. In contrast, quantitative filter paper is designed for applications requiring precise measurements and high purity, often used in gravimetric analysis. Both types cater to distinct laboratory needs, ensuring effective separation and reliable results in various scientific fields.</p></p>
