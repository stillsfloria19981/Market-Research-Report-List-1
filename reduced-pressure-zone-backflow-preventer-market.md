<p><p>A Reduced Pressure Zone Backflow Preventer (RPZ) is a vital plumbing device designed to eliminate the risk of contaminated water entering a clean water supply. It operates by maintaining a lower pressure in the zone between two independently acting check valves and an atmospheric vent. This ensures that, even under backflow conditions, potential pollutants cannot siphon back into the potable water system.</p><p>The Reduced Pressure Zone Backflow Preventer market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of water quality and safety regulations. Municipalities and industries are prioritizing the installation of RPZ devices to comply with stringent standards and protect public health. Additionally, urbanization and industrial development contribute to higher demand, as more complex plumbing systems require robust backflow prevention solutions.</p><p>The market for RPZ backflow preventers is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period, reflecting rising investments in infrastructure and water management systems.
