<p><p>The Highway Guardrail Board market is primarily segmented into two types: Two Wave Guardrail and Three Wave Guardrail. Two Wave Guardrails consist of a single, continuous wave design, offering basic protection and are typically used in lower traffic areas. In contrast, Three Wave Guardrails feature more complex, three-wave structures that provide enhanced energy absorption and are suitable for high-speed roads. Both types are essential for improving road safety, reducing the severity of vehicle impacts, and minimizing injury risks.</p></p>
