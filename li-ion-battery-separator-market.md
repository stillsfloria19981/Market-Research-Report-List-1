<p><p>The Li-ion battery separator market comprises several types, including weaving membrane, nonwoven membrane, microporous membrane, composite membrane, and others. Weaving membranes are made from interlaced fibers, providing structural strength. Nonwoven membranes consist of fibers bonded together, offering flexibility and improved performance. Microporous membranes have tiny pores for enhanced electrolyte retention and ion transport. Composite membranes combine materials to optimize thermal stability and mechanical properties, while "other" encompasses various specialized membranes tailored for specific applications or performance requirements in battery technology.</p></p>
