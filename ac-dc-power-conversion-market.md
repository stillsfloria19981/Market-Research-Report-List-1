<p><p>The AC-DC power conversion market is categorized into two main types: external AC-DC power and embedded AC-DC power. External AC-DC power solutions are standalone units that convert alternating current from mains supply to direct current, commonly used in consumer electronics and appliances. In contrast, embedded AC-DC power solutions are integrated within devices, providing direct current to internal circuits without the need for external converters. Both types cater to varying power requirements and applications across industries, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
