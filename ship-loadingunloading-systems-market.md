<p><strong>The Ship Loading/Unloading Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Ship Loading/Unloading Systems</li><li>Pneumatic Ship Loading/Unloading Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship loading/unloading systems market consists of two primary types: mechanical and pneumatic systems. Mechanical ship loading/unloading systems utilize traditional equipment such as conveyors, cranes, and grabs to handle bulk materials. These systems are efficient for heavy or bulk cargo like coal and grain. In contrast, pneumatic ship loading/unloading systems use air pressure to transport fine powders and granules, ensuring a clean and dust-free process. Both types cater to different cargo requirements and operational efficiencies in maritime logistics.</p></p>
