<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Laser Acupuncture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon source</li><li>He-Ne light source</li><li>Semiconductor laser</li><li>Nd: YAG laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser acupuncture market consists of various types based on the light source used. Carbon source lasers utilize carbon dioxide to deliver therapeutic energy, commonly used for skin treatments. He-Ne light sources, based on helium-neon gas, provide a low-intensity light effective for superficial therapies. Semiconductor lasers, known for their compact size and efficiency, are often employed in point-specific treatments. Nd:YAG lasers, using neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet, deliver deeper tissue penetration, making them suitable for more complex conditions.</p></p>
