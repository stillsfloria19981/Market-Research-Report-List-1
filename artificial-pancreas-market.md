<p><strong>The Artificial Pancreas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CTR System</li><li>CTT System</li><li>Threshold Suspended Device System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial pancreas market features three key types: </p><p>1. **CTR System (Closed-Loop Control)**: Integrates continuous glucose monitoring with insulin delivery for real-time glucose regulation.</p><p>2. **CTT System (Closed-Loop Therapy)**: Combines automated insulin delivery and monitoring, adjusting based on predictive algorithms to maintain optimal glucose levels.</p><p>3. **Threshold Suspended Device System**: Suspends insulin delivery when glucose levels drop below a set threshold, preventing hypoglycemia while allowing for manual adjustments. Each system aims to enhance diabetes management and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
