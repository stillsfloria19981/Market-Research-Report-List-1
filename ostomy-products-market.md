<p><p>The ostomy products market primarily features two main types of pouch systems: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags combine both the adhesive skin barrier and the pouch into a single unit, simplifying application and wear. In contrast, two-piece bags consist of a separate adhesive barrier and pouch, allowing for easier pouch replacement while maintaining the barrier. This segmentation caters to varying user preferences and needs, impacting comfort, convenience, and lifestyle choices for individuals with ostomies.</p></p>

<p><p>The ostomy products market includes three main applications: colostomy, ileostomy, and urostomy. Urostomy products serve individuals with urinary diversions, focusing on drainage bags and adhesives that manage urine output. These products enhance comfort, hygiene, and quality of life for ostomy patients.</p></p>
