<p><p>The Video as a Service (VaaS) market encompasses three primary types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud offers scalable video services through third-party providers, making it accessible to a wide audience. Private Cloud delivers tailored, secure video solutions for individual organizations, ensuring data privacy.
