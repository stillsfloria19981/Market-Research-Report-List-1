<p><strong>The Pediatric Digital X-ray System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pediatric Digital X-ray System market comprises two main types: CR (Computed Radiography) Tech and DR (Digital Radiography) Tech. CR Tech utilizes photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, offering a cost-effective solution for clinics transitioning from traditional X-rays to digital systems. In contrast, DR Tech employs flat-panel detectors for direct image capture, providing faster results and superior image quality. Both technologies cater to pediatric healthcare, enhancing diagnostic capabilities while ensuring minimal radiation exposure for young patients.</p></p>
