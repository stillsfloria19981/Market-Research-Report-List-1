<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cargo tank coating market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering lower emissions and easier cleanup, making them more environmentally friendly. They generally provide good adhesion and corrosion resistance. In contrast, solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, delivering superior durability and resistance to harsh chemicals, but often have higher VOC emissions. Both types cater to specific cargo requirements and environmental regulations, influencing their selection in various applications.</p></p>
