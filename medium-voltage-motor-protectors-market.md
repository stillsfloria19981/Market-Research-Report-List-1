<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Motor Protectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feeder Protection Relay</li><li>Differential Protection Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium Voltage Motor Protectors are essential for safeguarding electrical motors in various industrial and commercial applications. The Feeder Protection Relay focuses on monitoring and protection of the feeder lines connected to motors, ensuring safe operation by detecting faults such as overloads or short circuits. Meanwhile, Differential Protection Relays are designed to sense discrepancies between input and output currents, providing precise protection against faults within the motor itself. Both types enhance system reliability and prevent costly equipment damage.</p></p>
