<p><p>Electric Brake Systems (EBS) enhance vehicle safety and performance by utilizing electronic controls. The market is primarily divided into two types: Disc EBS and Drum EBS. Disc EBS features disc-shaped rotors that provide superior heat dissipation and braking efficiency, commonly used in high-performance vehicles. Conversely, Drum EBS utilizes a drum structure that encases brake shoes, offering reliable performance in commercial and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific vehicle requirements, influencing their adoption across various segments in the automotive industry.</p></p>
