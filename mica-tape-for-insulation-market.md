<p><p>Mica tape for insulation is primarily categorized into mica glass tape and mica polyester tape. Mica glass tape is made from a combination of mica and glass cloth, providing excellent thermal resistance and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, mica polyester tape incorporates polyester film, which enhances flexibility and adhesion, making it ideal for various insulation needs in electrical and electronic systems. Both types effectively insulate and protect against heat, ensuring reliable performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
