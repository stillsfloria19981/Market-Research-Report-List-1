<p><p>The brain health devices market encompasses various technologies for monitoring and assessing brain function. Electroencephalogram (EEG) captures electrical activity, while Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed imaging of brain structures. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers high-resolution images for diagnosing conditions. Magnetoencephalogram (MEG) detects magnetic fields produced by neural activity. Transcranial Doppler (TCD) measures blood flow in the brain, and Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitors pressure inside the skull. Cerebral Oximeters assess oxygen levels in brain tissue, highlighting their critical role in brain health evaluation.</p></p>
