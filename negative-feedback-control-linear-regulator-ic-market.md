<p><p>The Negative Feedback Control Linear Regulator IC market is primarily categorized into two types: Series Type and Shunt Type. Series Type regulators control output voltage by varying the resistance in series with the load, ensuring precise voltage regulation under varying load conditions. In contrast, Shunt Type regulators maintain output voltage by diverting excess current to ground, effectively shunting it away. Both types are essential in applications requiring stable voltage regulation, with each serving different operational needs in electronic circuits.</p></p>
